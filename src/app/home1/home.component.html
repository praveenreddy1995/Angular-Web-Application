How its Works

The silkworm (Bombyx mori) is the larva or caterpillar of the Bombyx mori moth. Silde for at least 5000 years in China. The moth is important because it makes the silk, and no longer lives in the wild. It is entirely dependent on humans. Silkworms eat mulberry leaves, and were native to northern China.
